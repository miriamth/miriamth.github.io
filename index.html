ELI FIELDSTEEL

Dr. Eli Fieldsteel is a composer specializing in music technology with a diverse history of cross-disciplinary collaboration. Fieldsteel’s music and research engages with the intersection between music technology and contemporary instrumental practice, focusing on topics such as human-computer improvisation,interactivity, and generative music. Fieldsteel is currently Assistant Professor of Music Composition-Theory at The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where he serves as the director of the Experimental Music Studios.

Fractus IV: Bonesaw (2012)
for trombone and quadraphonic electronics, commissioned by ASCAP/SEAMUS and written for Steve Parker. Premiered on April 18, 2013 at the SEAMUS Conference at McNally Smith College of Music, St. Paul MN.
